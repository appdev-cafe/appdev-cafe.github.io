OUR SERVICES

Computers

Use a PC or a Mac at our facilities for free! All you need is a valid CalNetID to use one.

Printing

Come and print in black and white or color using our computers or wirelessly with your device!

Scanning

Free hi-definition scanning is available with our computers at each of our facilities. All you need is a valid CalNetID to use one.

Software

Our computers have all the popular software that students need, such as Adobe CS6 and MATLAB.

Supply Lending

Need a pencil, tape, or even a charger? Come up to the front desk and see what's available to borrow!

Help Desk

Our trained student consultants can help you troubleshoot any technical problems that you have with your computer.

Searching for a campus job? Look no further.

We are currently hiring for the Educational Technology Services Student Consultant position. We are looking to hire knowledgeable, friendly, and dedicated students who have Macintosh or PC software and hardware experience for the Fall 2014 semester with possible summer hours.

Drop us a message!

Have any questions or concerns regarding our facilities? Just fill out the form to the left, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible! Feel free to leave any comments and suggestions as well.
